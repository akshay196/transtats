Track translation progress

In development of localised software we need to track translations per languages, native releases, repository releases, bugfixes and many more. Transanalytics collects templates, figure-out their translations, analyse statistics and generate reports.

Frequent sync with repositories

Transanalytics pulls data from code and translation repositories at regular intervals to ensure correct and latest data representations. Snapshots are also kept to manage diffs and growth analysis. On the top of all, it is configuration based.

Tap ship-to-locations readiness

Set location based milestones. Drive translation of your application for the locations carrying high demands, regulate its pace and ensure timely delivery. Experience the globalisation view.
